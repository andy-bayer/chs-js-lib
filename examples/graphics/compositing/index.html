Setting the globalCompositeOperation of an element will cause that operation to be used before drawing it. The options can all be seen on MDN.

In this example, a subclassed circle with globalCompositeOperation = 'destination-out' is used to act as an "eraser". When you click or tap the canvas, the circle's globalCompositeOperation becomes destination-in, and will act as whatever the opposite of an eraser is.
